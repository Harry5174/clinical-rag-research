## Classification roll-up

- `KEEP_WITH_CURRENT_EVIDENCE`: corpus descriptive counts only when accompanied by their limitation.
- `KEEP_BUT_REFRAME`: controlled hard-negative aggregate, filtered Phase 9 aggregate, hard-negative design, sidecar mechanism label, and existing limitation text.
- `CORRECT_METHOD_DESCRIPTION`: IndexFlatIP/exact-inner-product wording, L2 protocol wording, target-subject filtering, and sidecar provenance.
- `SOFTEN_SCOPE`: abstract, introduction, clinical implications, and conclusion performance/deployment framing.
- `REMOVE_UNSUPPORTED`: supplementary-material availability and undocumented 20-query tuning assertion.
- `REQUIRES_NEW_ANALYSIS`: router performance, sensitivity, comparative baselines, terminology-drift uncertainty, and negation-recency interpretation.
- `REQUIRES_SUPERVISOR_CLARIFICATION`: score-equivalence language, retention of the ``Virtual Graph'' label if material, and P38 target/intent.
- `DEFER_TO_REWRITE_SPEC`: all actual manuscript wording changes.
